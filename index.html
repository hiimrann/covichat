CoviChat

Are you in need of assistance? CoviChat was created to connect users with volunteers who are willing to help their fellow citizens in these hard times.

Our volunteers provide aid by sending food, water, medicine and other essentials to those who are unable to leave their homes or afford the items. Aid is also sent in the form of making welfare checks or helping contact the local Covid healthline for the elderly or anyone who might need it.
