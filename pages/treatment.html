Treatment

    Since the outbreak of Covid-19 no proper treatment has been discovered yet, nonetheless, the following practices can be followed as way of treatment if someone is exposed to coronavirus.
  • Call the health care provider or Covid-19 hotline to find out the procedures of how to get a test done.
  • Cooperate with contact-tracing procedures to stop the spread of the virus.
  • If testing is not available, stay home and maintain distance from others for 14 days.
  • While in quarantine, do not go outside or come in contact with people physically. Ask someone to deliver supplies.
  • Keep at least a 1 meter distance from others, even from family members.
  • Wear a medical mask to protect others.
  • Clean hands frequently with high alcohol-based soap or sanitizer.
  • Stay in a separate room from other family members, and if not possible, wear a medical mask to prevent the spread.
  • Keep the room well-ventilated.
  • If the room is shared, place beds at least 1 meter apart.
  • Monitor regularly for any symptoms for 14 days.
  • Contact health care provider immediately if have any of these danger signs such as: difficulty breathing, loss of speech or mobility, confusion or chest pain.
  • Stay positive by keeping in touch with loved ones by phone or online, and by exercising at home.
